50% of every ticket sold is donated to the Women's Initiative for Self-Employment

Women's Initiative for Self Employment, located at 1398 Valencia in the Mission is a Bay Area non-profit which provides high-potential, lower-income women the training, resources and on-going support to start and grow businesses. The business management training, technical assistance, and financial services we provide - in English and Spanish - improve the quality of life for the women we serve, their families and our communities.
